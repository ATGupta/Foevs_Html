DEEP ECOLOGY VS SHALLOW ECOLOGY

Shallow Ecology is a form of environmentalism which encourages measures of environment and its resources for the benefits of human beings.

While
Deep Ecology is a form of environmentalism which advocates the measures of protection of environment regardless of their effect on the welfare of people.

Recycling, saving energy, using hybrid cars, avoid using plastic bags, plant trees come under shallow ecology. Deep Ecology is mainly on changing the minds of people so that they change their way of treating it (exploitation), leaving behind our personal ego and family, we should think for other animals and trees.

(http://www.deep-ecology-hub.com/shallow-ecology.html)

"Earth First!": -

Earth First! is a radical environmental advocacy group. It emerged in South-Western United States. . It was co-founded on April 4th, 1980 by: -
     1. Dave Foreman
     2. Mike Roselle
     3. Howie Wolke

Members of Earth First! take an ecocentric view based in deep ecology, arguing that all living organisms are inherently valuable. Members work to defend the environment in a wide range of ways, from permitted marches to monkeywrenching.

(http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Earth_First!)

Monkeywrenching: -

Monkeywrenching is a form of sabotage [an act of damage] that focuses on creating serious economic damage, thus putting a temporary or permanent halt to activities that the perpetrator believes are undesirable.

Here, monkey wrenching is referred to the damage done to stop the unenvironmental activities.

(http://www.wisegeek.com/what-is-monkeywrenching.htm)
